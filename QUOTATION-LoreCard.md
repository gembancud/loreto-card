## Executive Summary

GA Solutions is pleased to submit this quotation for **LoreCard**, a comprehensive government benefit and voucher management system designed specifically for the Municipal Government of Loreto.

LoreCard digitizes and streamlines the distribution of social benefits and vouchers to residents, replacing paper-based systems with a secure, mobile-friendly platform that provides real-time tracking, audit trails, and multi-department management.

---

## System Overview

### Core Capabilities

**1. Resident Database Management**
- Complete beneficiary records with demographics and contact information
- Address management for all 16 Loreto barangays
- Profile photo capture and storage
- Income level tracking for eligibility assessment
- Status management (active, inactive, pending)

**2. Government Service ID Tracking**
- Tracks 9 government IDs per resident:
  - Voter's ID
  - PhilHealth
  - SSS
  - 4Ps (Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program)
  - PWD ID
  - Solo Parent ID
  - Pag-IBIG
  - TIN
  - Barangay Clearance
- Records ID numbers, issue dates, and expiry dates
- Visual badges for quick identification of eligible beneficiaries

**3. Benefit Configuration**
- Create unlimited benefit types per department
- Support for monetary values (peso amounts)
- Support for quantity-based benefits (goods/items)
- Activate/deactivate benefits as programs change
- Department-scoped for data isolation

**4. Voucher Management (Two-Step Workflow)**
- **Provider Role:** Issues vouchers to eligible beneficiaries
- **Releaser Role:** Confirms actual distribution of benefits
- Complete audit trail with timestamps
- Voucher status tracking: Pending → Released / Cancelled
- Historical records for compliance and reporting

**5. QR Code System**
- Unique QR code generated for each resident
- Downloadable QR cards for beneficiaries
- Mobile QR scanning for instant lookup
- Speeds up field operations and reduces errors
